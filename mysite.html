About myself

I recently graduated from Virginia Tech with Degree in Computer Science and minor in Mathematics.
At first I studied Mathematics because of precision and clarity that is inherent in it. In spite of what is popularly believed about this discipline, Mathematics is at its very core primarily concerned with the mode of justifying our claims. I found the intersection of Logic and Mathematics particularly interesting.
In my sophomore year I took my first Computer Science class, which was nothing more than introduction to the programming. This class had stirred a genuine passion for programming and Computer Science in general. What I found particularly interesting about Computer Science is the level of abstraction involved in programming and the extensive use of logic (not only in particular programming statements but in overall design). My interests beside Mathematics include:Computer Graphics , Mobile Development and Web Development. Here I would like to share my software I have managed to produce so far (my class projects and software solutions I have created on my own) and my other passions and interests.
