About myself

Currently I am an undergraduate student of Computer Science at Virginia Tech and my minor is in Mathematics..
At first I studied Mathematics because of precision and clarity that is inherent in it. In spite of what is popularly believed about this discipline, Mathematics is at its very core primarily concerned with the mode of justifying our claims. I found the intersection of Logic and Mathematics particularly interesting.
In my sophomore year I took my first Computer Science class, which was nothing more than introduction to the programming. This class had stirred a genuine passion for programming and Computer Science in general. What I found particularly interesting about Computer Science is the level of abstraction involved in programming and the extensive use of logic (not only in particular programming statements but in overall design). In other words Computer Science is the area which requires true mathematical mind-frame. So I ended up declaring Computer Science as my major but I still continue to take Mathematics classes , which allows me to further my interest and knowledge in Mathematics.
My interests beside Mathematics include:Computer Graphics in context Human-Computer Interaction and Web Development. Here I would like to share my software I have managed to produce so far (my class projects and software solutions I have created on my own) and my other passions and interests.
